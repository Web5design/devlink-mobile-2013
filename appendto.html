About appendTo

appendTo provides industry-leading front end software development and developer training services.

Our Services

Blazon is a cutting edge platform for delivering technical presentations and trainings. Blazon enables instructors and presenters to rapidly develop, iterate and share presentations with audiences around the world.

The ability to quickly develop presentations using Markdown and present example code with automatic syntax highlighting will quickly establish Blazon as a preferred platform for presenters and instructors worldwide. Blazon empowers presenters and instructors to push the quality of their sessions further than they’ve previously imagined possible.

Blazon is the next generation of appendTo’s presentation platform that has powered developer education sessions for some of the largest companies in the world including Adobe, Experian, Microsoft, Union Pacific and many more.
